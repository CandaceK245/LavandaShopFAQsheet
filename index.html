>Delivery/Pick Up:

We accept same-day orders and in-store pick up until 12PM Mt, Monday to Friday.
We accept orders until 2PM on Saturday for delivery on Sunday.
In-Store Pick up is available after 3PM Monday to Friday and 4PM on Saturdays and Sundays.

>Cost:

Our Same Day Delivery is $9.

>Order Tracking:

With our personal fleet we are able to offer live tracking, you will receive a link to our tracking website in your
confirmation email, this helps track where you are on the delivery schedule as well as a smaller delivery window so
you can make sure those receiving the delivery can be available, we do still offer contactless delivery.

>Business Instructions:

If we are delivering to a business in the notes at check out there is space available to leave a note for when the recipient
will be at the location or any special instructions to either be delivered directly to the recipient or it is ok to be left
at reception.

gifts delivered on time, every time. If we have missed the mark, please let us know and we will make it right!
